Jorune Bloodline

House Jorune Progenitor Welsh · Established 6th Century · Mythic Roots to Merlin

The original bloodline from which all later branches descend. House Jorune claims direct spiritual and academic lineage to early Brythonic magic and proto-wizard law. Considered foundational rather than politically dominant.

House Ashvale Branch Welsh · Established 13th Century · Gothic Eccentrics

Geographic & Historic Origin

  • Ancestral land: Wales
  • Current territory: Wales
  • Founder: Geraint o dŷ Jorune (Angelicized to Ashvale)
  • Current head: Norrix Ashvale

The Ashvale family is a notable pureblooded family in Wales... but perhaps not known for entirely positive reasons. "Eccentric", some neighbors would call them. "Mad", others would challenge. Many speculate the pure blood of their lineage has led to magical curses that affected the minds... Though, it wouldn't explain how those who enter the family outside of the blood bond are content within the Ashvale manor. Some have even posed that the family are all vampires, though there's never been any evidence to support this accusation. Regardless of what afflicts the family, many can agree the Ashvales are simply not normal.

Identity & Culture

  • Overarching value: Autonomy

Core Values

  • Core Value #1 - Individuality Without Shame: Eccentricity and unusual interests are welcomed by the Ashvales. "Strange" is not a flaw, but rather an act of bravery.
  • Core Value #2 - Loyalty to Kin: Ashvales are fiercely loyal to their family, whether by blood, marriage, or their own choosing.
  • Core Value #3 - Indulgence of the Obscure: Ashvales value that which is often overlooked by society, whether it's old magic, outdated traditions, or "odd" wizardry. Curiosity is cultivated and housed safely within their walls.

Taboos

  • Taboo #1 - Conformity for Approval: An Ashvale who dares hide their true nature to gain social favor or popularity is considered a traitor to their family.
  • Taboo #2 - Weaponizing Family Secrets: The Ashvale secrets are numerous and macabre; using them for leverage or blackmail is a grave violation.
  • Taboo #3 - Harm Without Purpose: Cruelty for the sake of cruelty is forbidden among Ashvales; they may be morbid and macabre, but they are not evil. Any harm must have clear intent, justification, and necessity.

Specialty Disciplines

  • Potioneering
  • Herbology
  • Anatomy & medical wizardry

Inter-Family Relations

Enemies

  • House Jorune: Progenitor family; conflicting views on the macabre and morbid.
House Greensong Branch English · Established 15th Century · Scholarly Aristocracy

Geographic & Historic Origin

  • Ancestral land: Wales
  • Current territory: England
  • Founder: Isambard ap Jorune (Angelicized to Greensong)
  • Current head: Rowland Greensong

The Greensongs were once very in tune with the Jorune bloodline; they favored and cherished knowledge. However, the Greensongs parted from their mother bloodline because the Jorune family was fixated on the preservation of old magic and following the ways of Merlin; the Greensongs desired a broader array of knowledge that could only be found outside the walls of their home, by travel and by cultural influence. They still cherish learning to this day, and act as keepers of knowledge in their various libraries and museums.

Identity & Culture

  • Overarching value: Knowledge

Core Values

  • Core Value #1 - Knowledge is Power: Knowledge is meant to be discovered, curated, and preserved whenever possible, so that it may be shared with future generations.
  • Core Value #2 - Objectivity in Study: Calm, objective observation and analysis is valued when documenting and curating knowledge; Greensongs are taught from an early age to compartmentalize and regulate their emotions for the sake of maintaining the integrity of the knowledge they're curating.
  • Core Value #3 - Knowledge is a Lifetime Pursuit: Learning doesn't end with formal education; it is a Greensong's duty to refine and hone their skills and career paths, and ultimately to contribute something of significance to pass onto the next generation.

Taboos

  • Taboo #1 - Willful Ignorance: Not knowing something is perfectly acceptable; the refusal to learn or change one's position after being presented with contradicting evidence is not.
  • Taboo #2 - Falsifying Records: Changing records to reflect personal bias or to falsify events is a grave sin. Even when it is inconvenient, dangerous, or painful-- knowledge must always be documented in all its truthfulness.
  • Taboo #3 - Hoarding Knowledge: Knowledge may be guarded to preserve secrets not meant for the public eye, but knowledge may never be hoarded for the sake of personal gain-- whether it's to blackmail others, assert dominance, or trying to gain social leverage.

Specialty Disciplines

  • History of Magic
  • Magical Law
  • Cartography & Charting

Neutrals

  • House Jorune: Mother bloodline; the two have made amends since their parting, and have historically worked with one another for the sake of knowledge.
