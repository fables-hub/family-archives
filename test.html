Jorune Bloodline

House Jorune Progenitor Welsh · Established 6th Century · Mythic Roots to Merlin

The original bloodline from which all later branches descend. House Jorune claims direct spiritual and academic lineage to early Brythonic magic and proto-wizard law. Considered foundational rather than politically dominant.

House Ashvale Branch Welsh · Established 14th Century · Gothic Eccentrics

A culturally eccentric offshoot known for ritualized domestic magic, funerary arts, and elaborate architecture. Harmless, insular, and deeply traditional.

House Greensong Branch English · Established 12th Century · Scholarly Aristocracy

Founded following migration into England. Greensong is defined by archivism, musical theory, and a legendary private library rivaling institutional collections.

House Moss Branch English · Established 13th Century · Rural Preservationists

A minor branch focused on land-magic, herbology, and stewardship of ancient groves. Politically quiet, magically stable.
